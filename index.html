My journey in software development began at ESTG, where I studied and built a strong foundation in programming and system thinking. As I grew in my studies, I understood that learning shouldn’t stop in the classroom. I took the initiative to expand my skills further, which led me to Santech Ltd. Throughout this journey, I have developed skills in web development and gained confidence in turning ideas into functional solutions.
